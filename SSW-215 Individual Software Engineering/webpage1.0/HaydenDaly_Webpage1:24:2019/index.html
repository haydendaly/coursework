Note From Hayden
January 26th, 2019

The reason I made a site about this author instead of myself is because I have recently become a large fan of his writing. I started with American Psycho when I first visited NY to tour colleges and the book made me feel very immersed within the city. My second experience with his writing was when I read Less Than Zero on my flight back home from my first semester at Stevens. The parallels between the main character of the book and me made the book incredible. It was based on a kid returning from his first semester of college on the East Coast to the San Fernando Valley in Los Angeles which was the exact situation I was in. Since then, over the winter break, I have read three more of his books and can't wait to finish the rest of them. That's the reason I chose his personal site for my assignment.
